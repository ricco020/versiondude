VersionDude

For several years this domain hosted Subversion (svn.versiondude.net) repositories for a handful of open-source projects in the web-standards and developer-tooling space — among them an early HTML5 parser, the Validator.nu checker, a Rails CMS, an OWL reasoner and an IRC client.

Those projects are the work of their respective authors and communities, and are maintained elsewhere today. VersionDude no longer runs a Subversion server; this site is an independent, original reference that documents each project and links to its current home. We claim no authorship of, or affiliation with, those projects.
